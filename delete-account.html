Request Account Deletion

GRAP allows you to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Contact us via email at yasirfwz17@gmail.com with the phone number linked to your account.
  2. We will process your request and delete the following data:
    • Phone number
    • Full name
    • Delivery address
    • Order history
  3. Any transactional records required for legal compliance may be retained for up to 2 years.
  4. After your request is completed, you will receive a confirmation email.
