Privacy Policy

Last updated:

GRAP (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates a quick commerce mobile application providing delivery of groceries and everyday essentials within 15–20 minutes in Colombo, Sri Lanka.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • Phone number (for OTP verification and account login)
  • Full name
  • Delivery address

b) Location Information

GRAP does not automatically track your device location. Users manually select their delivery location within the app using map functionality. The selected address is stored only for order fulfillment purposes.

c) Order Information

  • Order history
  • Purchased items
  • Delivery instructions

GRAP currently supports Cash on Delivery only. We do not collect or store any card or online payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your identity via OTP
  • Process and deliver orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services
  • Prevent fraud and misuse

3. Third-Party Services

GRAP uses third-party services strictly for app functionality, including mapping services provided via the Google Maps SDK.

4. Data Sharing

We may share necessary information with delivery personnel to fulfill your order. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

5. Data Security

All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted using secure HTTPS connections.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.
